📚 How to Use

Fast workflow (recommended)

  1. Paste your sentences in the box.
  2. Check “Has hints (one per line below)” and paste matching hints in the new box underneath.
  3. Click “Parse Text & Click Words to Blank”.
  4. Click words you want to blank — they are auto-added to the word bank.
  5. Click ✅ Build Game.
  6. Hints will appear under each sentence.

Other features

  • Word Bank stays on the right quarter.
  • 🔍 Zoom button cycles text size (5 levels) — now fixed!
  • Numbered or Paragraph display mode.
