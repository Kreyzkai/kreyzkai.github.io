About This Site

Teaching Tools — CMarasigan for Kasai-Minami High School

This is a private collection of browser-based tools for generating classroom games and worksheets. Everything runs offline in your browser — no internet connection needed once the files are loaded.

What each tool does

Each numbered tool (001–014) lets a teacher enter content and generate a ready-to-use student activity — either as a game played on-screen or a printable worksheet.

How to use

Click any card to open that tool. Fill in your content, preview, and download the student file. Student files are standalone HTML — just open and play.
