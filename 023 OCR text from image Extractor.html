📄 How to Use
🔑 Getting Your Free API Key (one-time setup)
  1. Go to aistudio.google.com/apikey
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Click "Create API key"
  4. Copy the key (starts with AIza…)
  5. Paste it in the field above and click Save

The key is stored only in your browser. No payment info needed.

📁 Uploading
  • Click or drag & drop a file into the upload area
  • Supports PDF, JPG, PNG, WebP
  • iPhone photos: share as JPG
  • PDFs are limited to 3 pages per run (free tier)
📊 Output Formats
  • Excel (.xlsx) — grids, seating charts, tables, schedules
  • Word (.doc) — notices, letters, text-heavy documents

Gemini decides which format best fits the content automatically.

⚠️ Privacy note: On the free tier, Google may use uploaded content to improve their models. Avoid uploading documents with sensitive personal data (student ID numbers, etc.). General school notices and seating charts are fine.

Layout will be structurally faithful, not pixel-perfect. Designed to be translation-ready.
