PPTX Text and Animation Lister Guide
Purpose

This tool reads a .pptx file and pulls out every piece of text in the exact order animations play during your slideshow — plus a full breakdown of every animation event per click.

Nothing is uploaded. Everything runs inside your browser. Your file never leaves your device.

How to use
1
Drop your .pptx file onto the upload area, or click it to browse
2
The tool processes every slide automatically — no settings needed
3
Switch between Text List (what text appears when) and Event Tracker (what effect fires on each click)
4
Download as TXT or CSV — the active tab determines what gets exported
Event Tracker columns
entr
Entrance — shape or text flies/fades/appears onto the slide
exit
Exit — shape or text leaves the slide
emph
Emphasis — shape pulses, spins, changes colour etc. without leaving
path
Motion Path — shape moves along a drawn path
Trigger types

On Click — fires when the presenter clicks. Starts a new click count.

With Previous — fires simultaneously with the event above it. Same click.

After Previous — fires automatically after the event above finishes. Same click.
